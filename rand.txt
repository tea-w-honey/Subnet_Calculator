[E/D]`"`n`nif ($action -notmatch `"^[EdD]$`") {`n`tWrite-Error `"Invalid choice. Wrong password or corrupted data.`"`n`t`t`tbreak # Stop processing immediately if the password is wrong to prevent damage`n`t`t}`n`t}`n`tWrite-Host `"`nSuccess! All files decrypted.`" -ForegroundColor Green`n}`n`n# Clean up memory`n$aes.Dispose()`n[System.GC]::Collect()" > tmp.ps1`n
